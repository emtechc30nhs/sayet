SAYET 

    Sayet is a sitio of Barangay Caliking. It is devided into two, the Upper Sayet and Lower Sayet. It is along the Halsema Highway and can be found at Km. 25 just 1 hour to 1 hour and 30 minutes travel from Baguio .

    Arabica coffee is the main product of the sitio because of it's wide plantations of Arabica coffee. It is the primary source of income of most residence. Many of the residences have it on their backyard and most of the residences have stress effort to widen the Coffee Industry. Some of them even attended seminars to widen their knowledge about Arabica coffee and upon returning they share it to their fellow farmers.

    An cooperative has been established that aims to unite the coffee farmers, help one another and to have a coop that members can rely to. It is also established to produce premuim Arabica coffee beans through proper handling of coffee beans from harvesting to roasting.

    Though the industry is in progress, farmers still face problems that can be solve if that support from the public and authorities is present.
